Animated Movie History

An animated movie or cartoon, is made up of a series of slightly different drawings of people, animals, and objects that make them appear to move.

The Simpson's show has always kept up with the times, switching from their original cel animation style to digital ink and paint animation in 2004, and now using early access to the latest Adobe technology to animate their characters live on air.

Oldest Running TV Series Is Animated

The animated series has been on the air for over 30 years and is currently in its 30th season. The Simpsons made its debut on December 17, 1989, and has become the longest-running primetime scripted show in the U.S., as well as the longest-running animated series in history. The famous show has led to the creation of newly made movies. This website outlines my favourite one, the 2007 Simpson - The Movie.
